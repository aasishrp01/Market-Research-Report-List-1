<p><p>The vehicle smart mirror market consists of two main types: interior digital mirrors and outside digital mirrors. Interior digital mirrors are installed inside the vehicle and offer various functionalities such as displaying video feeds from the rearview cameras, providing navigation information, and integrating with other vehicle systems. On the other hand, outside digital mirrors are designed to replace traditional side mirrors and offer enhanced visibility and safety features, such as blind spot detection and integrated cameras. Both types of smart mirrors contribute to improving the overall driving experience and increasing safety on the roads.</p></p>
