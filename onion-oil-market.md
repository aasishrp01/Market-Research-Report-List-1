<p><strong>The Onion Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steam Distillation</li><li>Solvent Extraction</li><li>Supercritical Extraction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Onion oil, a product derived from onions, can be obtained through different market types such as steam distillation, solvent extraction, and supercritical extraction. Steam distillation involves heating the onions to release the oil, which is then condensed and collected. Solvent extraction utilizes chemical solvents to extract the oil from the onions. Supercritical extraction uses carbon dioxide under high pressure to separate the oil from the plant material. These methods offer varying levels of efficiency, yield, and quality, providing options for manufacturers and consumers in the onion oil market.</p></p>
