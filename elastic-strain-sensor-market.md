Other types of strain sensors include inductive, piezoelectric, and optical sensors, which measure strain using different principles. These different types of strain sensors offer a range of options for accurately measuring and monitoring strain in various applications, including structural health monitoring, aerospace, automotive, and robotics industries, among others.</p></p>
