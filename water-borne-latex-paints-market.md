<p><p>Water-borne latex paints are categorized into four types based on their specific uses. Interior water-borne latex paints are designed for painting indoor surfaces such as walls and ceilings. Universal water-borne latex paints, on the other hand, can be used for both interior and exterior surfaces. Anti-fouling water-borne latex paints are specifically formulated to prevent the accumulation of marine organisms on boats and ships. Lastly, antibacterial water-borne latex paints contain substances that help inhibit the growth of bacteria and are commonly used in hospitals, clinics, and other healthcare facilities.
