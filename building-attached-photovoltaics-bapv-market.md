<p><p>Building Attached Photovoltaics (BAPV) market types consist of Type I and Type II. This type allows buildings to generate electricity while utilizing their existing structures. Type II BAPV represents the integration of photovoltaic systems during the construction phase of a building. This type is designed to seamlessly incorporate solar panels into the building's structure, providing a sustainable energy solution from the start. Both types contribute to the renewable energy sector by harnessing solar power in different stages of building development.</p></p>
