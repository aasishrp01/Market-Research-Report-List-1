Pearlescent pigments display a pearly or iridescent effect, created by layering multiple colors on top of each other. Other types of special effect pigments include fluorescent pigments, thermochromic pigments, and photochromic pigments, which offer unique characteristics such as color-changing abilities under specific conditions or enhanced visibility under UV light.
