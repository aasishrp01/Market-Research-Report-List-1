<p><p>Continuous extrusion blow molding machines are used for the production of hollow plastic bottles, containers, and other similar products. These machines can be classified into two types: shuttle machines and rotary wheel machines. Shuttle machines have a stationary extruder and a moving mold, which enables the production of bottles of various shapes and sizes. On the other hand, rotary wheel machines have multiple molds mounted on a rotating wheel, allowing for continuous production of bottles at a high speed. Both types of machines are commonly used in the plastic manufacturing industry.</p></p>
