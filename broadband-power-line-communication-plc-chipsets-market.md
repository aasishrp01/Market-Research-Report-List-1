<p><p>Broadband Power Line Communication (PLC) chipsets are used to transmit data over existing electrical power lines. These chipsets are categorized into two types: standalone and hybrid. </p><p>Standalone PLC chipsets are designed solely for power line communication, allowing for efficient and reliable data transmission without the need for additional technologies or networks. They are commonly used in applications like smart grid management and home automation systems.</p><p>Hybrid PLC chipsets, on the other hand, integrate power line communication with other technologies like wireless or Ethernet. This combination enables more versatile communication options and improved coverage, making it suitable for applications such as smart homes and industrial automation.</p></p>
