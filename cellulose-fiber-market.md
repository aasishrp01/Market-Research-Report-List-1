<p><p>The cellulose fiber market can be classified into two main types: natural cellulose fibers and man-made cellulose fibers. Natural cellulose fibers are derived from natural sources such as cotton, flax, hemp, and jute. They are known for their biodegradable and eco-friendly properties, making them popular in the textile and clothing industry. On the other hand, man-made cellulose fibers are produced through chemical processes using wood pulp or other natural polymers. They are widely used in industries like textiles, paper, and packaging due to their versatility and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
