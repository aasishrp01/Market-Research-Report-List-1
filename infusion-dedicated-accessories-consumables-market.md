Enteral infusion pumps are used to administer nutrition and medications directly into the digestive system. Syringe infusion pumps allow controlled delivery of small volumes of fluids. Ambulatory infusion pumps are portable devices for continuous medication administration. PCA pumps enable patients to self-administer pain medication. Implantable infusion pumps are surgically implanted devices for long-term medication delivery.</p></p>
