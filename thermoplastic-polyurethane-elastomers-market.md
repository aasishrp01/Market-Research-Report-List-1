<p><p>The Thermoplastic Polyurethane Elastomers (TPU) market can be categorized into three types: Polyester-Based, Polyether-Based, and Others. Polyester-Based TPUs are made from ester-based polyols and are known for their excellent mechanical properties, high temperature resistance, and enhanced chemical resistance. Polyether-Based TPUs, on the other hand, are produced using ether-based polyols, offering exceptional low-temperature flexibility, hydrolytic stability, and resistance to microorganisms. The "Others" category includes TPUs that are made using different raw materials and have distinctive properties, such as specialty additives or enhanced flame retardancy.</p></p>
