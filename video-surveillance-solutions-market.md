<p><p>Video surveillance solutions can be classified into two main types: IP Video Surveillance and Analog Video Surveillance. </p><p>IP Video Surveillance relies on digital technology, utilizing internet protocols for transmitting video data. It offers high-quality video, remote access, and advanced analytics capabilities. IP cameras can be easily integrated with other systems, such as access control and alarm systems.</p><p>On the other hand, Analog Video Surveillance relies on analog technology, using coaxial cables for transmitting video signals. While it is a more traditional approach, it still provides reliable video surveillance capabilities at a lower cost.</p><p>Both types of solutions have their own advantages and can be tailored to meet specific security needs.</p></p>
