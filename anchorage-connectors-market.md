Permanent connectors are intended for long-term installations, while temporary connectors are used for short-term or portable applications. Other types may include mobile or adjustable connectors. Appearance-wise, Type A connectors are often eye-bolt style, Type T connectors resemble a tie-off adapter, and Type D connectors are typically used with horizontal lifelines. These market distinctions help match the right connector for specific safety needs.</p></p>
